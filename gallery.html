SPIC – MACAY VIRASAT

The Assembly ground of Hills High School, Surat ,on slightly raised platform Mysore A Chandankumar kept the audience spell bound over two hours with his musical concert. He began his recital with the customary ‘Mahaganapatim’ in Natti. Chandan Kumar played popular kirtis based in easily detectable ragas such as Ranjani and Hamsadhwani . He was accompanied by talented and experienced Vidwan Shri G S Ramanujan on Mridangam and Vidwan Mathur R Srinidhi on Violin.
