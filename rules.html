Rules & Regulations

Primary Section (2021–22)(GBSE)

				» Next academic year will commence from 09/06/21 (Monday).

				» On June 9th June 2021 the Parents of new admitted child have to come to drop and pick their child from the school. 
				    Bus / Rickshaw will come for old students.

				» School timings : From : 09/06/21 [ Monday to Friday] 8:00am to 11:00am Every Saturday there will be holiday.

				» Students must reach the school before 8:00am. Late coming students will be sent back home and in that case school 
				    will not take any responsibility.

				» Duration to meet Principal 
				   » Monday to Friday 11:00 am to 12:00 pm
				   » Saturday: 12:00 pm to 2:00 pm

				» Parents Will not be allowed to meet class teacher during working hours.

				» For new admitted Students Temporary I Card will be provided. I card must be filled up by the parents and attached 
				    to the child′s uniform, when he\she comes to the school on very first day.

				» Exam will be taken as per grading system.

				» Parents have to pay the fees for new session on or before 30/04/21.If not paid, the child′s admission will be
				    considered as cancelled.

				» Parents I card is compulsory whenever they come to school. It is also necessary when they bring the child to the school 
				    in the morning and take back after the school.

				» Attendance of Parents is compulsory in Parents teachers meeting and in the meeting with the management. 
				    Late coming parents will not be allowed to attend the meeting.

				» Fees must be paid regularly. After the 1st month of each installment Rs 5/- Per day and in the 3rd month Rs.10/- Per day 
				    will be charged as Penalty. First installment must be paid before school re-opening otherwise the child will not be 
				    allowed to sit in the classroom.

				» Parents are strictly informed not come to school to take the child back during school hours.

				» Parents may get excuse from the Penalty for genuine reason if they inform in written to the administrator or principal, 
				    before the date of fees payment.

				» If the student remains absent during exam, re-test will not be conducted.

				» Change in Address, contact no must be informed in two written copies to receptionist.(For office/ principal )

 				» Don′t send the ward if he / she is not well. Half day leave will not be given.

				» Parents must send one empty steel Tiffin box and spoon everyday with the child.

				» If students leave Bus / rickshaw service without informing the school they will have to pay fees for the entire year.

				» Result of 5 std to 8 std will be declared on 12/04/21 Saturday. Between 1:00pm to 2:00pm.

				» Uniforms are available at Omgarments, Vimalgarments, & Vardhman Garments.
